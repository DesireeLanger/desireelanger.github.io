• Although the class II superfamily phylogeny is not entirely clear, the dimeric GlyRS is part of subclass IIa alongside ProRS, SerRS, ThrRS, and HisRS (Gomez et al. 2020, Valencia-Sánchez et al. 2016, Perona et al. 2012). With the exception of SerRS, the anticodon binding domains of subclass IIa are homologous and reside at the C-terminal (Wolf et al. 1999).
