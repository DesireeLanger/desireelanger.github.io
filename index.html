AARS Online

The aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases (AARS) are a large group of enzymes which execute the computational rules of the genetic code by attaching amino acids to their cognate tRNA. AARS Online documents their sequence, structure, and function across a taxonomically representative sample of enzymes. The AARS catalytic domains fall into the Class I and Class II superfamilies, as summarised by the evolutionary tree below.
